FAQ

Common questions.

What is Focus Battery?

Focus Battery is a focus tracking app for iPhone that estimates your daily mental energy based on your sleep, chronotype, and screen time — so you know exactly when to do deep work and when to rest.

Is it useful for ADHD?

Yes. Many users with ADHD find it especially helpful because focus energy is harder to predict and manage. The app surfaces your best focus windows so you can plan around them rather than forcing productivity at the wrong times.

Does it read my personal health data?

Focus Battery reads sleep data from Apple Health with your permission. It never writes to HealthKit, never stores your data on external servers, and never shares it with third parties. Everything stays on your device.

Is Focus Battery free?

The core focus tracking features are free. An optional Focus Battery Pro subscription unlocks advanced insights and personalization. No ads, ever.

Which devices are supported?

Focus Battery is available on iPhone and requires iOS 16 or later. Download it from the App Store.

How accurate is the focus forecast?

The forecast improves as the app learns your patterns over time. It combines sleep quality, your chronotype, and usage data to build a model tailored to your personal energy rhythm.
